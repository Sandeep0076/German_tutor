## Common Mistakes to Watch For:

1. **THE GOLDEN RULE:** Remember - ONLY masculine changes in accusative (der→den, ein→einen)
2. **Position 2 Rule:** The verb is ALWAYS in position 2, regardless of what comes first
3. **Wo/Woher/Wohin:** Wo = location, Woher = origin, Wohin = destination
4. **Nicht vs Kein:** Use kein for nouns with indefinite articles, nicht for everything else
5. **Doch:** Use "doch" (not "ja") to contradict a negative question
