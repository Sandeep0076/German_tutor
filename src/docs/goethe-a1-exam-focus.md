# Goethe A1 Exam Focus - Reading Section (Lesen)

## Overview

The reading section of the Goethe A1 exam consists of three parts (Teil 1, 2, and 3) with a total of 15 questions.

---

## Teil 1 (Part 1) - Questions 1-5

### Format
- **1 large text** (email/notice/message)
- **5 questions** based on that single text
- Answer format: **Richtig** (Correct) or **Falsch** (False)

### Example Structure
You read one email or message (like the example with Karin's email about the train arrival in Hannover), and then answer 5 true/false questions about the content.

**Example:**
- Question 0 (Beispiel): "Lis Zug kommt aus Hannover." → Richtig / Falsch
- Question 1: "Lis Zug kommt nach halb eins an." → Richtig / Falsch
- Question 2: "Karin wartet den ganzen Vormittag vor der Auskunft." → Richtig / Falsch
- (And so on for questions 3-5)

---

## Teil 2 (Part 2) - Questions 6-10

### Format
- **5 questions** (6-10)
- **Each question has its own 2 different texts/websites** (option **a** or **b**)
- Choose which text (a or b) provides the information needed for that specific question

### Important Structure Details
- **NOT** 2 websites for all 5 questions
- **EACH** of the 5 questions has its own unique pair of websites/texts
- Total: 5 questions × 2 websites each = 10 different websites/texts

### Example Structure
Each question presents a scenario and two different websites. You must choose which website (a or b) has the information you need.

**Example Question 0 (Beispiel):**
- Scenario: "Sie wollen wissen: Regnet es in Deutschland?"
- Option **a**: www.openair.de (Open-Air concert information)
- Option **b**: www.dwd.de (German weather service)
- Answer: Choose **b** (weather service has weather information)

**Example Question 6:**
- Scenario: "Sie möchten mit dem Schiff auf dem Rhein fahren."
- Option **a**: www.schiff-ruedesheim.de (Hotel-Pension "Schiff")
- Option **b**: www.bingen-ruedesheimer.de (Bingen-Rüdesheimer Rheinschiffe)
- Answer: Choose **b** (Rhine ships, not a hotel)

**Example Question 7:**
- Scenario: "Sie suchen einen Computerkurs."
- Option **a**: www.vhs-berlin.de (Adult education center with courses)
- Option **b**: www.computer-shop.de (Computer store)
- Answer: Choose **a** (VHS offers courses)

*Note: Questions 8, 9, and 10 would each have their own different pair of websites.*

---

## Teil 3 (Part 3) - Questions 11-15

### Format
- **5 questions** (11-15)
- For each question: **1 text/notice**
- Answer format: **Richtig** (Correct) or **Falsch** (False)

### Example Structure
Each question presents a scenario and one notice/sign. You determine if the statement is correct or false based on the notice.

**Example:**
- Question 0 (Beispiel): "Zum Deutsch Lernen gehen Sie in die Beethovenstraße 23."
  - Notice shows: "SPRACHZENTRUM - Das Sprachzentrum ist umgezogen. Sie finden uns jetzt in der Beethovenstr. 23"
  - Answer: **Richtig** / Falsch

- Question 11: "In der Sprachschule können Sie etwas zu essen kaufen."
  - Notice shows: "In der 10-Uhr-Pause bekommen Sie an der Rezeption ein Frühstückspaket: Belegte Brötchen und Getränke für 2 Euro."
  - Answer: **Richtig** / Falsch

---

## Summary

| Part | Questions | Format | Answer Type |
|------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Teil 1 | 1-5 (5 questions) | 1 large text → 5 questions | Richtig/Falsch |
| Teil 2 | 6-10 (5 questions) | 1 question → 2 texts (choose a or b) | a or b |
| Teil 3 | 11-15 (5 questions) | 1 question → 1 text | Richtig/Falsch |

**Total: 15 questions**
