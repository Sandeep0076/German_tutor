## Implementation Strategy Options

### Option A: Maintain 50-Day Structure (Aggressive Compression)
**Pros:**
- No timeline changes
- Existing student schedule preserved

**Cons:**
- Requires compressing Phase 5 exam prep
- Less breathing room between complex topics
- Risk of overwhelming students in Phase 3

**Approach:**
- Add critical topics as .5 days (Day 18.5, 21.5, etc.)
- Compress Phase 5 from 10 to 6 days
- Reallocate 4 days to Phase 3 expansion

### Option B: Expand to 56-Day Structure (Recommended)
**Pros:**
- Proper spacing for complex topics
- Adequate exam preparation time
- Better learning retention with review days
- Follows pedagogical best practices

**Cons:**
- Longer overall timeline
- Need to update marketing materials

**Approach:**
- Expand Phase 3 from 10 to 16 days (as recommended)
- Add 3 mid-phase review days
- Maintain robust Phase 5 exam preparation
- Total: 56 days (8 weeks)

**RECOMMENDATION: Option B - 56-Day Structure**

## Critical Missing Topics - Detailed Specs

**Day 21.5: Reflexive Verbs - Present Tense**
- **Sections:**
  1. Introduction to Reflexive Verbs
  2. Accusative Reflexive Pronouns (mich, dich, sich...)
  3. Common Daily Routine Verbs
  4. Sentence Structure with Reflexives
- **Examples:** 15-20 sentences
- **Homework:** 30-35 questions
  - Recognition (10): Identify reflexive verbs
  - Production (12): Fill in reflexive pronouns
  - Application (8-13): Create sentences about daily routine

**Day 22.5: Reflexive Verbs - Advanced**
- **Sections:**
  1. Perfect Tense with Reflexive Verbs
  2. Dative Reflexive Pronouns (sich die Zähne putzen)
  3. Emotional/Social Reflexive Verbs (sich freuen, sich treffen)

- **Examples:** 15-20 sentences
- **Homework:** 30-35 questions
  - Recognition (10): Perfect tense forms
  - Production (12): Dative vs Accusative
  - Application (8-13): Describe weekend activities

### 2. Subordinating Conjunctions (Days 38.5-39)

**Day 38.5: Subordinating Conjunctions - Reasons & Opinions**
- **Sections:**
  1. Verb-to-End Rule Explanation
  2. weil (because) - Expressing Reasons
  3. dass (that) - Reporting & Opinions
  4. Comma Placement Rules
- **Examples:** 15-20 sentences
- **Homework:** 35-40 questions
  - Recognition (12): Identify verb position
  - Production (15): Sentence transformation
  - Application (8-13): Explain reasons for actions

**Day 39: Subordinating Conjunctions - Conditions & Questions**
- **Sections:**

- **Homework:** 35-40 questions

- Complete sentences
- Conjugate verbs
- Apply rules

- All phases in single file
- Becoming unwieldy
- Difficult to maintain

### Proposed Modular Structure

```

├── syllabus.ts (main export + types)
├── syllabusPhases/
│   ├── phase1.ts (Days 1-10)
│   ├── phase2.ts (Days 11-20)
│   ├── phase3.ts (Days 21-36)
│   ├── phase4.ts (Days 37-45)
```

- Easier to maintain
- Parallel editing possible
- Better code organization
- Faster IDE performance
- Clear ownership of sections
